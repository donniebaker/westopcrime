As you may have noticed, on our wrime website, the blog section still is very active. If you are searching for a guest post publication, containing fresh content of criminality related research or ideas to give our visitors a nice read, you may as well read on.

On our blog, we try to publish contributions with the latest scientific criminiality report news, the very best insights and new trends and world of crime and legal. New fraud insights most surely have their place on my website. We are always on the lookout for delightful insights and authors to have the ability to share their knowledge in the field of fighting crime with our readers. If you would like to contribute a guest post to our blog, please find our guidelines in full below and contact our editorial team at donniebaker79@post.com if you’re ready to get published.

Guest post writing guidelines

Here are our writing requirements: • We only accept original content. This means that the guest post you send us cannot be published elsewhere on the internet. • The style and tone of your article must fit our blog. We exclusively use a casual tone in articles that make our readers smile when going through the article. Only well researched writing of the best quality will be accepted. • Please use headings to organize your article’s content. You may use a question-answer type of headings if you choose to do so. • Only posts with containing over 1000 words of content will be accepted. • You post may contain 1 or 2 outbound links to your company’s website. Links can only be placed in the body text of the article. • Please add a headshot, a short bio and (if you prefer to do so) links to your social networks. • Please attribute data and quotations to any source you reference from outside the post. • Once published, we expect you to share the article across all your social networks, media networks and mailing lists.

What we can’t accept as guest articles

• Any topics that have been previously covered on the blog. We would advise you to a thorough search on our entire blog before submitting. • Any type of post that is made up for link building purposes only. • An article that contains too much promotion to you, your company or organization • Offensive and inaccurate content. • Too critical articles that will not please our readers will be rejected also.

 

The Blog Post Submission Process

If you think your guest post submission is up to the guidelines described above, you may contact us for submission purposes. Please do so by e-mailing your article to donniebaker79@post.com. Don’t forget to mention your full name, organization, reasons you want to contribute to our blog and how you found us. Please also mention references to earlier guest posts you have published in the restaurant niche. Donnie will get back to you as soon as possible with the next steps, including more detailed writing instructions and some agreements to adhere to.

Conclusion

• Only the best quality standards are good enough for articles you submit • No republishing of your guest post on you own blog or other media (LinkedIn, Medium, …) • Our team reserves the right to edit and adapt your guest blog content and title as we see fit, and update it in the future for accuracy and comprehensiveness. • We’re happy to welcome you as a guest writer, if you add credibility and state-of-the-art content to our blog.
